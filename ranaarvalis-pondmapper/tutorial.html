FrackFinder PA - Project Moor Frog:

SkyTruth needs your help to locate ponds that might be associated with drilling and fracking in Pennsylvania.

We're going to show you aerial imagery of Marcellus Shale wellpads across Pennsylvania. Your mission (should you choose to accept it) is to mark the center of all the ponds you see around these wellpads.

How to select ponds

Click on all ponds you see within the red rectangle. They will get marked with a red "X". Click "Done" once you have marked them all, or if you can't see any. You can zoom and pan the map to see more details.

Click again on the same spot to remove a marker you accidentally placed in the wrong place.
