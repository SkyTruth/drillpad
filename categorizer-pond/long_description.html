FrackFinder is a crowdsourced skytruthing project to find, map and track drilling and fracking (drilling for natural gas using hydraulic fracturing) across the United States.

SkyTruth is using satellite and aerial imagery to identify fracking sites, track activity on those sites, and map the extent of the visible surface impact from every frack site we can see.

Crowdsourcing helps us create brand new data on fracking while providing contributors (that's you!) a new perspective on the issues through participating in the mapping process.
