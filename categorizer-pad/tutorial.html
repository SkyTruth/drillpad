TUTORIAL - Well Pad (Part I)

A well pad is a site that has been cleared, leveled-off, and covered in gravel to allow drilling and fracking to occur. Most well pads you will see will be roughly rectangular and appear close to the center of the image, have a visible access road and be and partially or completely clear of vegetation.
