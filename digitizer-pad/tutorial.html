Pad shape outlining

Hi! This is an application to help estimate the footprint of fracking operations for SkyTruth.

The application is really simple. It loads a map with satellite imagery and asks you to draw an outline around any drill pad you see within a certain area.

A simple rectangular site where the outline is pretty obvious. Draw a rectangle around the area, but don't include the access road.

Another simple rectangular site.

Please watch the instructional video below on how to mark outlines.
